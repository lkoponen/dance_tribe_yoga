What is Dance.Tribe.Yoga?

No, this is NOT our traditional yoga- it's a dance party on our mats! Come join a group of women AND men who are interested in having a fun, sweaty time by getting down with a fast-paced, tribal-dance and plyometric inspired yoga workout!

We will gyrate, twist, roll, and twerk together for a core-intensive cardio fest! Let's put our moves and grooves to a mix of high-energy tunes! Bring your towel, mat, water bottle, and willingness to get wild on your mat! Music will probably be explicit! No heat, WE'LL bring that.
